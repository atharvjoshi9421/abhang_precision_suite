Wirecut Service

Wire EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) cutting is our specialty. We provide ultra-precise wirecut services for hardened tool steels, carbide, and exotic alloys. Our CNC wirecut machines achieve tolerances of ±0.005mm, making them ideal for die & mould components, precision gears, and intricate profiles that conventional machining cannot achieve.
